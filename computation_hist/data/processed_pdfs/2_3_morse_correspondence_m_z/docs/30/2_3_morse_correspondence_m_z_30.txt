The rate for the three day conference is $40 .00,
or $15.00 per day, and $8.00 for a half day. Pre-

registration by mail is suggested to aid the spon-
sors in planning and smooth operation,
Program Participants
All participants on the Program will have pre-
registered and should tell the registering official
this when checking in for the conference.
Meals
The facilities of the Iowa State Memorial Union
are available to course enrollees. All meals
which are a part of the Program are included in
the fee. Extra tickets purchased are $2.50 each
for dinners and $1. 25 for luncheons,
Parking
Cars may be parked in the Short Course parking
lot one block northeast of the Memorial Union.
This parking lot is especially for campus visitors.
The Vehicle Code of Iowa applies to the campus.
A municipal metered parking lot is located

three blocks southwest of the Memorial Union,

east of Stanton Avenue just south of the tracks.
