Data Processing Seminar for University Professors - August 5th -
August 9th, 1957 - Endicott, New York:

The purpose of this Seminar is to bring together
professors and instructors who are currently
responsible for courses in the Business Data
Processing field, It is intended that those in
attendance will be able to discuss the content of
existing courses, new applications, and new
courses,

Introduction to Data Processing for University Professors - August 19th
to August 23rd, 1957 - Endicott, New York:

The purpose of this class is to introduce professors
and instructors to the problem of teaching and
organizing classes in Business Data Processing.
